FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick, detailed answers regarding our features, pricing estimation logic, and destination scopes.

How does the AI assistant plan my trip?
WanderAI maps your text commands and parameters against predefined location graphs, price tiers, and activity catalogs. It models the ideal itinerary flow matching your preferences.
Are the budget calculations accurate?
Yes, our estimates cover local transit, average hotel bookings, meal pricing, and entry cards. These reflect standard rates within seasonal variance indexes.
Can I customize the generated itineraries?
Absolutely! In the flight assistant widget, you can ask for adjustments such as "make the Goa plan more relaxing" or "add adventure activities to Munnar" to update recommendation listings.
What destinations are supported?
Currently, WanderAI includes detailed flight logs and deep localized database plans for Goa, Ooty, Munnar, Kodaikanal, Pondicherry, and Manali, with additional global destinations coming soon.
